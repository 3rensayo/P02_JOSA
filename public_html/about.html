ABOUT JOSA

OUR STORY

To deliver the ultimate in casual dining.

Born on the West Coast and growing across Canada, JOSA offers the best in global cuisine using local, fresh ingredients served in a contemporary setting.

Our service is attentive, warm and unpretentious. Our people are passionate about delivering food and experiences that will keep our guests coming back again and again.

Canada's 100 Best Restaurants - 70th / canadas100best.com

Five Must-Sees in Vancouver - Food and Drink / National Post

CHIEF

Alvin has two rules when it comes to cooking: keep it simple and use quality ingredients.

Chef Alvin Christopher is one of Canada’s most recognized and celebrated chefs. At JOSA, Chef Christopher leads the culinary team, blending his talent for creating elegant, globally-inspired cuisine with his passion for simple dishes using fresh, sustainable ingredients.

COMMUNITY

Giving back is part of our mission. That’s why we participate in programs and partnerships aimed at improving the local community.

We love being a go-to gathering place in our community and we believe strongly in giving back where we live and work. Over the past five years, JOSA has donated more than $1 million and countless services to a vast array of causes.
